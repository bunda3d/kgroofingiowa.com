Origins Raised On Roofing

KG Roofing is named after owner Kyle Grell. Kyle spent years learning the roofing trades while working for a family business, eventually deciding to start his own venture.

Experience

KG Roofing has the expertise to seam-weld membranes on an enormous factory roof one day, then turn around a single-family tear off & re-shingle the next. This versatility comes with professionalism; meaning KG works hard, safely, and runs a tidy worksite with cleanup.

Professionalism

This professionalism extends to working with customers: from understanding what the job requires, to ensuring it's all done to satisfaction.
